Dil mera ruk janda tak ke, searching for the light
Tu meri jaan ban gayi ae, my only spark
Sheeshe vich tu hi disdi mainu, glowing in the dark
Mere saah ruk jande ne kudiye, losing my breath
Tera nasha mainu chadhya is qadar, love until death
Kach chon bahar aaja tu samne, into the bright moon
Mere hathan vich hath pa lai apna, hope I see you soon
